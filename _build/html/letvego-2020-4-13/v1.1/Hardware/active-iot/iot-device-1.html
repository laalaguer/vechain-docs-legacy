} [/block] 1. Temperature warning and tracking 2. Temperature recording and storing 3. Encrypted transmission of temperature data 4. Device identity verification 5. Compressed storage and transmission of temperature data 6. Mass memory, which can store more than 138,000 temperature records 7. The minimum collection interval can be set to 1 second 8. High measurement accuracy, ±0.3 °C accuracy between 0 °C ~20 °C and ±0.4 °C accurancy between -20°C ~ 40 °C 9. High resolution, which is 0.1°C. 10.
